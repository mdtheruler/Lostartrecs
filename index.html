About Lost Art Recs

Lost Art Recs is an independent record label founded by a lifelong Hip Hop enthusiast born in 1985. Growing up with the culture since the age of 10, I have explored all four elements of Hip Hop – from writing and rapping to DJing and graffiti – but my true passion has always been collecting and preserving the music itself.

Our mission is simple: to honor the artists and make sure timeless records that shaped underground Hip Hop are available again – legally, officially, and in a way that preserves their legacy.
